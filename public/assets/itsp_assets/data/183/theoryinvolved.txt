<p>The following describes the working of the bot:</p><p>-The GPS system informs the bot of its current location.</p><p><img style="width: 320px;" src="http://api.ning.com/files/kzWDJ8OLEAv3Dv1zjBluw9HYYdfpGyAmzusjlEHzDLazYVOsx4edsoSqBWL39y81liaAKEwrYaNNdRuf5Cu8Lg__/SDIM0688.jpg"></p><p>-This information is used to calculate the bearing to a pre programmed waypoint using formula.</p><p><img style="width: 247px;" src="http://img.dxcdn.com/productimages/sku_198733_1.jpg"> -       Once the bearing is available,the digital compass and motors together align the bot in the direction of the waypoint.</p><p><img style="margin: 0px 0px 10px 10px; width: 218px; float: right;" alt="" src="http://www.alselectro.com/images/Geared-Motor.jpg"></p><p>-Any obstacles are detected by the ultrasound sensors.</p><p><img style="width: 291px;" src="http://www.robosoftsystems.co.in/wikidocs/images/archive/8/84/20130108065629!HC-SR04.jpg"></p><p>There are<span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0);"> two sensors</span>, <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0);">one which always faces the target, on the servo motor and another which always points the direction the bot moves</span>. When the senor pointed towards the target senses an obstacle, the bot turns till the fixed sensor does not detect any obstacle and the other sensor turns to face the obstacle. The bot moves straight. Then, when both the sensors are clear, the bot recalculates bearing and moves towards the target. The bot keeps moving this way till it reaches the destination.</p><p>The bot stops once it is within 10 meters of the waypoint</p><p>Along the path to the waypoing, the <strong>heading keeps changing</strong>. In the bot, the heading is recalculated every 5 secs(approx.) and the bot reoriented.</p>
