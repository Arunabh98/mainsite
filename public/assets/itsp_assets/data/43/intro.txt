<p>Our Team Name is Big Hero. Our team ID is  69.</p><p>We have made the "Pen Plotter". In this project, the user will be able to write a certain document or draw sketches through a bot, that too in his own handwriting!</p><p>The user has to input the images of the letters in his own handwriting which would prove useful for writing the specified document.</p><p>Our device processes the images and write the content of document on a piece of paper in user's handwriting. </p><p>Image processing using MATLAB is used in our project. Communication of MATLAB and motors are done through Arduino. </p>
