<p>We had planned to make a self balancing bot which can balance itself on two wheels. The basic idea of the project is when the bot tilts to one side, the motors are accelerated to that side in such a way that it can avoid falling in that side. Same case happens for the opposite side too. Thus bot gets
balanced stays vertical.</p><p><a href="https://www.google.co.in/search?q=self+balancing+robot&amp;espv=2&amp;biw=1366&amp;bih=667&amp;tbm=isch&amp;tbo=u&amp;source=univ&amp;sa=X&amp;ved=0CCkQsARqFQoTCK7sus2nkcYCFYTnpgodxSEA0g#imgrc=UAJ5JjLIbvh0FM%253A%3BLk5_qWw-6o7WVM%3Bhttp%253A%252F%252Fmedia.techeblog.com%252Fimages%252Fselfbalancing_2.jpg%3Bhttp%253A%252F%252Fwww.techeblog.com%252Findex.php%252Ftech-gadget%252Ffeature-japanese-engineer-builds-self-balancing-robot-table%3B450%3B327"></a></p><p><img src="http://www.balanduino.net/images/balanduino1.png"><img src="http://hacknmod.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/VertiBOT.jpg"></p><p><img src="https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcRNztWH9wX5oHvoefw4OZZn4AEHVm-rwC8S4m6npyTtGpZZjooJWA"></p>
