<p>A robotic arm is a type of
mechanical arm, usually programmable, with similar functions to a human arm;
the arm may be the sum total of the mechanism or may be part of a more complex
robot. The links of such a manipulator are connected by joints allowing either
rotational motion(such as in an articulated robot) or translational (linear)
displacement Introduction</p><p>Our main motive was to build
a system that takes the "finger , palm , elbow movements" of a hand
and moves the fingers of another hand/mechanical model of a hand identically.</p><p>Here are two of the videos that inspired us..</p><p><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PIhmBszYHOU">https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PIhmBszYHOU
