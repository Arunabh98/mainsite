<p style="margin-left: 40px;"></p><p style="margin-left: 20px;">                         We manged to make a non-invasive system for driver drowsiness detection embedded on safety glasses.</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">          Following factors were used to predict driver drowsiness:</p><p style="margin-left: 120px;">1.<strong>Heart rate</strong></p><p style="margin-left: 120px;">2.<strong>Coefficient of variation of R-R intervals (experimental stage)</strong></p><p style="margin-left: 120px;">3.<strong>Eye blink Duration</strong></p><h3 style="margin-left: 40px;"><strong>Heart rate:</strong></h3><p style="margin-left: 40px;">Normal Human Heart rate (resting) ranges between 60-100 bpm</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">Heart rate is known to fall from normal resting heart rate during drowsiness</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">Heart rate was measured with PPG waveform</p><h3 style="margin-left: 40px;"><strong>Coefficient of variation of R-R intervals:</strong></h3><p style="margin-left: 40px;">During experimentation with various parameters related to heart rate,we tested the Coefficient of variation of R-R intervals which seems to rise during drowsiness</p><h3 style="margin-left: 40px;"><strong>Eye blink Duration:</strong></h3><p style="margin-left: 40px;">Normal human eye blink duration is around 400 ms (average) which increases considerably during drowsiness.</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">Eye blink was detected with TCRT5000 sensor</p><h3 style="margin-left: 40px;"><strong>Hardware and their roles:</strong></h3><p style="margin-left: 40px;">(Arduino was choosen to allow faster prototyping)</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">Arduino NANO: Fixed on the glasses,measures Heart rate,Eye blink duration,sends data to arduino UNO</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">Arduino UNO :measures Coefficient of variation of R-R intervals,controls alarm/LCD system</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">TCRT5000:used to detect eye blink</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">Pulse sensor: for obtaining PPG waveform by attaching it to earlobe</p><h4 style="margin-left: 40px;"><strong>Alarm System</strong>:Includes a LCD for data display,buzzer for alert during danger</h4><p style="margin-left: 40px;">We also managed to make PPG signal visualizer using processing ,which shows Heart rate,R-R intervals,PPG waveform </p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">This can be useful for analysis in biological fields.</p>
